I want to be remembered for being DIFFERENT

I am a passionate, self-driven young leader with an interest in Fashion, Business, Technology and Research. Professionally, I am a fashion designer and the Creative director of Abbie Poise, a sustainable Afrocentric clothing brand. I am open-minded and always curious to learn new things and explore.

  • Hi, I'm Ganiyat

    Hey there! I am Ganiyat aka Abbie. I am from the brown roof city (Ibadan) in Nigeria. I had interest in science and technology from a tender age so I attended a pure science high school, Wesley College of Science (the best in the country üòä). I am currently pursuing a degree in Computing.

    Before ALU, I had a diploma in Science Laboratory Technology from the Gateway ICT Polytechnic, Nigeria.

  • ALU Journey

    Like the sky, ALU's ad followed me everywhere (It still does üòÅ). After ignoring it for months, I decided to start the application on the deadline day. The campus tour and student ventures videos I saw online doubled my interest in studying at the institution.

    The application process was rigorous because I had to write three essays, fill several forms and send emails to the admission support team in a day.

    My joy knew no bounds when I received the acceptance email. It was an answered prayer for me as it was my prayer point for months. Few months later, it was time to travel to the beautiful Island. I tested positive to COVID-19 a day before the supposed travel date.

    I missed my flight and I had to treat myself for a week before I went for another COVID-19 test which returned negative.

  • Experience in Mauritius

    Mauritius is a beautiful and peaceful country.

    Even though some citizens are racists, most of the citizens are nice, accommodating and always ready to help.

  • Social Life

    I am not really sociable so I spend most of my time indoors.

    The places I visit most are markets and fabric stores.

  • Ideas for the Future

    I want to infuse fashion and technology so as to make wearable technology that will help humanity.
